Food App Prototype: I have used bootstrap,React for the frontend part. For storing the data, I have made use of firebase for database.

The application offers a range of functionalities, including the ability to browse through a variety of food items. Administrators have the privilege to seamlessly add new items, modify prices, and remove existing ones. Additionally, users can conveniently purchase their preferred food items through a designated link, enhancing the overall user experience. This comprehensive feature set caters to both administrative tasks and user engagement, making the application a versatile and user-friendly platform for food-related interactions. The App consist of features like viewing food items. Admin can add new food items , edit price and delete items. User can buy food items via this link.
